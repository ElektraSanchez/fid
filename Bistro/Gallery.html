Gallery

Not only is our food good, it's also good-looking! Our patrons often stop to admire our fare with a quick Instagram before digging in. We've collected a few of our favorite shots here.

Our Baked Goods


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves that are not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our lovingly made burgers. We grind our own locally-sourced organic beef and turkey, so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Side of Fries


Golden chrispy delights, indulge yourself in the warmth of such delicacies. Our golden brown signature gourment fries are a perfect side to add to any item, or perfectly good on their own!

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fisherman to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an old favorite with our regulars.

Southern Fried Chicken


A crunch with flavour. These freshly fried chicken tenders are hand-breaded and fried to perfection. Our secret seasoning of herbs and spices gives it a unique flavour. A delicious meal for yourself or to share with friends.

Tabbouleh


Don't let it intiminate you, our Tabbouleh is a delight! It has fresh ingredients, straight from our local farmers. It's a Levantine salad composed of many greens, such as parsleys, mint, tomatoes and wheat. Mixed with extra virgin olive oil and a squeeze of lemon. It is freshly prepared each time, you'll taste the difference.
